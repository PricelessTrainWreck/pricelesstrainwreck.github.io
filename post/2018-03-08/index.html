Yeah, sometimes we refer to this method of development as the ‚Äúlightning strike‚Äù. It‚Äôs definitely the absolute worst way to do things and have any level of predictability for your game. Why it‚Äôs called lightning strike is because on the rarest of occasions - the designers find something that suddenly feels really good and then the game is saved by that hail mary/lightning strike moment.
